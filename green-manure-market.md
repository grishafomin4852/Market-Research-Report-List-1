<p><p>The green manure market consists of two main types: leguminous and non-leguminous green manure. Leguminous green manure refers to plants from the legume family, such as clovers, peas, and beans, that fix atmospheric nitrogen into the soil, improving its fertility. Non-leguminous green manure includes plants like buckwheat, rye, and oats that provide organic matter and increase soil biomass. Both types of green manure play a crucial role in soil fertility by enhancing nutrient levels, preventing erosion, and promoting sustainable farming practices.</p></p>
