<p><p>The IoT Identity Access Management market comprises three main types: Identity Governance, Access Management, and Directory Services. Identity Governance involves managing and monitoring user identities, roles, and access rights within the IoT network. Access Management focuses on granting or denying access to IoT resources based on user authentication and authorization. Lastly, Directory Services involve storing and retrieving user information, such as usernames and passwords, to facilitate secure access and management within the IoT ecosystem. These types collectively ensure proper identification, access control, and user management within IoT networks.</p></p>
