<p><p>Weighted bar market offers various types of bars catering to different fitness needs. Standard bars are versatile and commonly used for general strength training. Olympic weightlifting bars are designed for dynamic lifts, with specific markings for grip and flexibility. Trap bars are uniquely shaped for deadlifts and reduce stress on the lower back. Safety squat (yoke) bars have a yoke-shaped design to provide stability and alleviate pressure on the shoulders during squats. Cambered bars have a curved shape to enhance range of motion and target specific muscle groups. Swiss bars offer multiple grip options for variations in pressing exercises. Curl bars, also known as EZ-bars, are designed for targeting the biceps during curls.</p></p>
