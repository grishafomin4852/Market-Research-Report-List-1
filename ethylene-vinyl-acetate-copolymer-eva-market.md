The increasing demand for EVA in the automotive and packaging industries is a major driver for market growth. The automotive industry uses EVA in the production of various components such as bumpers, fenders, and door panels due to its lightweight and impact resistance properties. Additionally, the expanding use of EVA in the packaging sector for films and sheets, particularly in food packaging applications, is further fueling market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing adoption of renewable energy sources, particularly solar power, is another key factor driving the demand for EVA.
