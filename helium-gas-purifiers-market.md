<p><p>The helium gas purifiers market can be broadly classified into two types based on pressure - low pressure and high pressure market. In the low pressure market, purifiers are designed to remove impurities from helium gas at pressures below atmospheric pressure. These purifiers are commonly used in applications such as gas chromatography and leak testing. On the other hand, the high pressure market involves purifiers that are specifically designed to remove impurities from helium gas at pressures above atmospheric pressure. These high pressure purifiers are typically used in industries such as aerospace, electronics, and nuclear energy.</p></p>
