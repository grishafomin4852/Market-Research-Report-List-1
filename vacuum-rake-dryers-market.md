<p><strong>The Vacuum Rake Dryers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum rake dryers are classified into two market types: Type I and Type II. Type I vacuum rake dryers are widely used in various industries for drying granular materials. They feature a single shaft with rakes that rotate to enhance the drying process. Type II vacuum rake dryers, on the other hand, are designed specifically for sticky and viscous materials. They incorporate double shafts with counter-rotating rakes to prevent material build-up and enable efficient drying. These market types cater to different drying needs, providing effective solutions for different types of materials.</p></p>
