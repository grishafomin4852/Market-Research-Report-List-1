<p><p>Alcohol Breathalyzer and Drug Testing are two types of tests that are conducted to determine the presence and levels of alcohol or drugs in an individual's system. These tests are commonly used by law enforcement agencies, workplaces, and healthcare facilities to ensure safety and compliance.</p><p>Alcohol Breathalyzer is a device that measures the alcohol content in a person's breath. It works by detecting the concentration of alcohol vapors in the breath sample and converting it into a digital reading. The results are then used to determine if a person is intoxicated or has consumed alcohol beyond the legal limit.</p><p>Drug testing, on the other hand, involves analyzing biological samples such as urine, blood, saliva, or hair to detect the presence of drugs or their metabolites. It is performed to screen individuals for drug use or to monitor compliance with treatment programs.</p><p>The market analysis of the Alcohol Breathalyzer and Drug Testing industry indicates a steady growth trajectory.
