<p><p>The performance elastomer market comprises various types of elastomers that offer exceptional performance characteristics. Ethylene vinyl elastomers combine the properties of rubber and plastics and find applications in automotive and electrical industries. Fluoroelastomers exhibit exceptional resistance to chemicals and extreme temperatures, making them suitable for aerospace and oil and gas industries. Silicone elastomers are known for their excellent heat resistance and biocompatibility, making them widely used in medical and electrical sectors. High-performance thermoplastic elastomers combine the properties of thermoplastics and elastomers and find applications in automotive, consumer goods, and construction sectors.</p></p>
