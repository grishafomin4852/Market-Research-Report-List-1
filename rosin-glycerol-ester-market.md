<p><p>The rosin glycerol ester market consists of various types, including rosin diglyceride, rosin triglyceride, and others. Rosin diglyceride refers to a type of rosin ester that is formed by the combination of one molecule of rosin with two molecules of glycerol. Rosin triglyceride, on the other hand, is formed by the combination of one molecule of rosin with three molecules of glycerol. The "others" category incorporates any rosin glycerol esters that do not fall under the specific definitions of rosin diglyceride or rosin triglyceride.</p></p>
