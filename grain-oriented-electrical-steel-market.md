<p><p>Grain Oriented Electrical Steel (GOES) is categorized into three main market types based on its magnetic properties and manufacturing processes. High Magnetic Strength GOES is designed to generate a stronger magnetic field, making it suitable for power transformers and generators. Conventional GOES is the most common type, used in various electrical equipment due to its balanced magnetic properties. Domain Refinement GOES undergoes additional annealing to enhance its magnetic performance, making it ideal for high-voltage power transmission applications. These market types cater to specific demands for different electrical applications.</p></p>
