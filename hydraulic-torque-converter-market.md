<p><p>A hydraulic torque converter is a device used in vehicles to transfer power from the engine to the transmission. There are two main types of hydraulic torque converters: single-stage and multistage. </p><p>A single-stage hydraulic torque converter only has one set of impellers and turbines, which allows for a simpler design and lower cost. It is commonly used in smaller vehicles and machinery.</p><p>On the other hand, a multistage hydraulic torque converter has multiple sets of impellers and turbines, allowing for higher torque multiplication and improved efficiency. It is typically found in larger vehicles and heavy-duty equipment, where more power transfer is required.</p></p>
