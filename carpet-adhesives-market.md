Acrylic adhesives provide a balance between strength and flexibility, making them suitable for a wide range of carpet installations. Vinyl adhesives are known for their resistance to chemicals and moisture, making them ideal for areas prone to spills. Other types of adhesives include specialty formulas designed for specific carpet materials or installation requirements.</p></p>
