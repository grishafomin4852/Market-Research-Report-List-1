<p><p>The UAV piston engines market is divided into two main types: 4-stroke type and 2-stroke type. The 4-stroke type engines, as the name suggests, complete four strokes - intake, compression, combustion, and exhaust - in one revolution of the crankshaft. These engines are known for their higher fuel efficiency and quieter operation. On the other hand, 2-stroke type engines complete these four strokes in just two revolutions, resulting in a simpler design, lighter weight, and higher power-to-weight ratio, making them ideal for smaller UAVs.</p></p>
