<p><strong>The AiP (Antenna in Package) Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LTCC</li><li>HDI</li><li>FOWLP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AiP (Antenna in Package) Module market comprises three main types: LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic), HDI (High-Density Interconnect), and FOWLP (Fan-Out Wafer Level Packaging). LTCC technology uses ceramic substrates to integrate the antenna into the module, offering good performance and reliability. HDI integrates the antenna using high-density interconnects to achieve compact size and enhanced performance. FOWLP technology is based on wafer-level packaging, enabling integration of the antenna directly into the module, resulting in smaller footprint, improved performance, and reduced time-to-market for wireless communication devices.</p></p>
