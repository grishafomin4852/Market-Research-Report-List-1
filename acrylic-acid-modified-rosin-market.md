<p><p>Acrylic Acid Modified Rosin (AAMR) market includes various types such as AR120 and AR127. AR120 AAMR is suitable for pressure-sensitive adhesives, ink, and coating applications due to its excellent solubility and compatibility. It provides tackiness and adhesion properties to these formulations. On the other hand, AR127 AAMR is a high softening point resin suitable for hot melt adhesives, sealants, and soldering flux. It offers improved heat resistance, bonding strength, and durability in these applications. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and properties of adhesives, inks, coatings, and other related products.</p></p>
