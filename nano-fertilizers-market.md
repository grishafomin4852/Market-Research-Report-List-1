<p><p>Nano fertilizers are a type of agricultural products that are created by incorporating nanoparticles to enhance the nutrient content and efficiency of traditional fertilizers. Zinc nano fertilizers contain zinc nanoparticles, which aid in the growth and development of plants. Boron nano fertilizers, on the other hand, are enriched with boron nanoparticles to improve plant metabolic functions. Nano aptamers are nanoscale bio-molecules that can bind to specific plant targets. Nano porous zeolite is a nanoparticle-based fertilizer that improves nutrient absorption by plants. Carbon nano tubes are cylindrical carbon molecules that enhance nutrient delivery to plants. Lastly, there are other nano fertilizers available in the market that utilize different nanoparticles for various purposes.</p></p>
