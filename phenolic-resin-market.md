<p><strong>The Phenolic Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resol Resin</li><li>Novolac Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phenolic resin market is divided into two types: resol resin and novolac resin. Resol resin is a type of phenolic resin that is thermosetting and offers excellent heat resistance, durability, and chemical resistance. It is often used in the production of plywood, laminates, and paper impregnation. On the other hand, novolac resin is another type of phenolic resin that is also thermosetting but offers superior mechanical strength and resistance to high temperatures. It finds applications in industries like automotive, electronics, and aerospace for manufacturing components requiring high strength and heat resistance.</p></p>
