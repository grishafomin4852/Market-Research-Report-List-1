<p><p>The photovoltaic materials market can be classified into two types: crystalline materials and thin film. Crystalline materials are composed of solid crystals and account for the majority of photovoltaic installations. They are further divided into monocrystalline and polycrystalline materials, with the former being more efficient but costly. Thin film materials, on the other hand, are composed of multiple layers of semiconductor materials and have lower efficiency but are more cost-effective. Both types play a significant role in harnessing solar energy for electricity generation.</p></p>
