<p><p>The trace heating cable market is classified into four types: self-regulating heating cable, constant wattage heating cable, mineral insulated heating cable, and skin-effect heating cable. </p><p>Self-regulating heating cable adjusts its heat output based on temperature changes, offering energy efficiency and protection against overheating. Constant wattage heating cable provides a consistent heat output throughout its length, making it suitable for long pipelines. Mineral insulated heating cable offers high temperature resistance and is commonly used in industrial applications. Skin-effect heating cable is used for heating long metal pipes by inducing an electric current on the pipe's surface, ensuring uniform heat distribution.</p></p>
