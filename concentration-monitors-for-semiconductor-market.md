<p><p>Concentration Monitors for Semiconductor Market Types are categorized into two main types: Stand-alone and Portable. Stand-alone concentration monitors are designed for permanent installation within a semiconductor production facility. They continuously monitor and analyze gas concentrations in real-time, providing accurate data for process control and optimization. On the other hand, portable concentration monitors are compact and handheld devices used for on-site gas concentration measurement in various stages of semiconductor fabrication processes. They offer flexibility and ease of use, allowing technicians to monitor gas levels in different areas of the production facility.</p></p>
