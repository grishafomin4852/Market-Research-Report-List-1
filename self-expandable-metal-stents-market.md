<p><p>Self expandable metal stents (SEMS) are small, flexible metallic tubes that are used to treat narrow or blocked blood vessels or passages in the body. These stents can be inserted into the affected area and then expanded using a forceps or a balloon to open up the passageway and restore normal blood flow or relieve obstructions. SEMS are commonly used in the treatment of conditions like gastrointestinal strictures, biliary strictures, and esophageal cancer.</p><p>The future outlook of the self expandable metal stents market is promising. The market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecasted period due to several factors. Firstly, the rising prevalence of gastrointestinal disorders and cancers worldwide is anticipated to drive the demand for SEMS. Additionally, the aging population and increasing adoption of minimally invasive procedures are also expected to contribute to market growth.</p><p>Technological advancements in SEMS, such as the development of biodegradable stents and the introduction of drug-eluting stents, are likely to further propel market growth. These innovations aim to improve the efficacy and reduce the risks associated with stent implantation. Moreover, the growing awareness among healthcare professionals regarding the benefits of SEMS is expected to boost market demand.</p><p>However, there are certain challenges that may hinder market growth. The high cost of these stents and the risk of complications, such as stent migration or tissue overgrowth, can pose challenges to widespread adoption. Additionally, the availability of alternative treatment options, such as surgical interventions or non-metallic stents, may also impact market growth.</p><p>In conclusion, the self expandable metal stents market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecasted period. However, challenges like high costs and alternative treatment options may pose obstacles to market expansion.</p></p>
