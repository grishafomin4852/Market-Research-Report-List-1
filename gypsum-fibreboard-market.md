<p><p>The gypsum fibreboard market is categorized into three types: regular, moisture resistant, and fire resistant. Regular gypsum fibreboard is commonly used in dry indoor areas for general construction purposes. Moisture resistant gypsum fibreboard is designed to withstand high humidity environments such as bathrooms and kitchens. It has additives that make it resistant to moisture absorption. Fire resistant gypsum fibreboard contains additives that enhance its fire resistance properties, making it suitable for areas requiring higher fire protection standards, like commercial buildings and fire-rated partitions.</p></p>
