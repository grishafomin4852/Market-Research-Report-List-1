<p><p>The cosmetic colorants market can be categorized into two types: crystal and powder market. Crystal colorants are in a solid, crystalline form and are often used in lipsticks, eyeshadows, and nail polishes. They provide a vibrant, shimmery effect to the products. On the other hand, powder colorants are finely ground pigments and are commonly used in foundations, blushes, and powders. They offer a matte or satin finish to the cosmetics. Both crystal and powder colorants are widely utilized in the cosmetic industry to enhance the aesthetic appeal of various beauty products.</p></p>
