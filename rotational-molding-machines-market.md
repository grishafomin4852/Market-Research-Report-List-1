<p><p>Rotational molding machines are specialized equipment used in the manufacturing process of rotational molding, which is a technique wherein molten plastic material is rotated inside a mold to achieve the desired shape of a product. <p><p>Rotational molding machines are used in the manufacturing process of creating hollow plastic products. There are different types of rotational molding machines available in the market. The first type is rock and roll machines, which rotate the mold in a rocking motion, enabling uniform coating of the mold's inner surface. Clamshell machines have a two-part mold that opens and closes like a clamshell, allowing for easy loading and unloading of the product. Shuttle machines have a shuttle system that moves the mold in and out of the heating chamber. Lastly, there are other types of rotational molding machines that don't fit into these specific categories.</p></p>
