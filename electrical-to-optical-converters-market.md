<p><p>Electrical to optical converters are devices that convert electrical signals into optical signals. These converters are categorized based on the type of fiber optic cables they support, which are multi-mode and single mode. Multi-mode converters can transmit signals through multiple paths simultaneously, allowing for shorter distance communications. Single mode converters, on the other hand, transmit signals through a single path, enabling longer distance communications. The choice between these converter types depends on the specific requirements of the communication system, such as distance and bandwidth.</p></p>
