<p><p>Bending Beam Load Cells are used to measure the force or weight applied on them. They come in various types such as Piezoelectric Load Cell, Hydraulic Load Cell, and Pneumatic Load Cell. A Piezoelectric Load Cell converts the applied force into an electrical signal, while a Hydraulic Load Cell uses fluid pressure to measure the force. On the other hand, a Pneumatic Load Cell utilizes air pressure to determine the force. All these load cells play a crucial role in industries requiring accurate weight measurements.</p></p>
