<p><p>Wheat straw pulp is a type of paper pulp that is derived from wheat straw, the crop residue left after the grain has been harvested. It is an eco-friendly alternative to wood pulp, as it utilizes agricultural waste and reduces the need for deforestation. Wheat straw pulp is widely used in the production of various paper-based products such as packaging materials, tissues, and specialty papers.</p><p>The wheat straw pulp market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing environmental concerns and the demand for sustainable products are driving the market growth. Wheat straw pulp offers benefits such as lower carbon emissions, energy savings, and reduced water consumption compared to traditional wood pulping methods. These factors are attracting manufacturers to shift towards wheat straw pulp for their paper production needs.</p><p>Additionally, the stringent government regulations and initiatives promoting sustainable practices are further fueling the market growth.
