<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Foundry additives market primarily consists of three types: organic additives, inorganic additives, and hybrid additives. Organic additives are derived from natural sources and are used to improve sand properties, such as reducing moisture content and increasing strength. Inorganic additives, on the other hand, are mineral-based and are used to enhance properties like thermal stability and permeability. Hybrid additives combine both organic and inorganic additives to provide a combination of benefits from both types. These additives are crucial for improving the quality and performance of foundry molds and cores during metal casting processes.</p></p>
