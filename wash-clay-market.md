<p><p>Wash Clay is primarily classified into three market types, namely the Wet Technology, Dry Technology, and Vapour-phase Technology. The Wet Technology involves using clay mixed with water to create a slurry, which is then applied for cleaning purposes. The Dry Technology entails using clay that has been dried and processed into a powdered form, allowing it to be easily mixed with water or other solvents. The Vapour-phase Technology involves utilizing clay in a gaseous state to remove contaminants. Apart from these, other market variations may also exist, offering distinct applications and benefits.</p></p>
