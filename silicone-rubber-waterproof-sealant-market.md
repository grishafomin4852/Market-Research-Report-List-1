<p><p>The silicone rubber waterproof sealant market can be categorized into three types: acetic acid sealant, alcohol sealant, and amine sealant. Acetic acid sealants, also known as acid-cure sealants, release acetic acid during the curing process and are commonly used for various applications. Alcohol sealants use alcohol in the curing process and provide excellent adhesion and durability. Amine sealants utilize amine compounds for curing and offer resistance to various chemicals. These different types of sealants cater to diverse needs in different industries while providing waterproofing properties.</p></p>
