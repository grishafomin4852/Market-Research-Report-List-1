<p><strong>The Rubber Masterbatch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPDM-based Type</li><li>SBR-based Type</li><li>NBR-based Type</li><li>Natural Rubber-based Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber masterbatch is a chemical additive used in rubber manufacturing to enhance its properties. There are different types of rubber masterbatch available in the market. EPDM-based type is made from ethylene propylene diene monomer and is known for its excellent weather resistance and electrical insulation properties. SBR-based type is derived from styrene butadiene rubber and offers good abrasion resistance and durability. NBR-based type is made from nitrile butadiene rubber and provides oil and fuel resistance. Natural rubber-based type is derived from natural rubber and offers excellent elasticity and tear resistance.</p></p>
