<p><p>The Central Processing Units (CPUs) market consists of various types of CPUs, including the BGA (Ball Grid Array) and CSP (Chip Scale Package) markets. BGA CPUs are integrated circuits that have solder balls on their underside for easy mounting onto a circuit board. They require a high level of expertise for installation and are commonly used in high-performance applications. On the other hand, CSP CPUs are smaller in size and have a compact design, enabling them to be directly mounted onto the circuit board. They are generally used in mobile devices and applications where space is limited.</p></p>
