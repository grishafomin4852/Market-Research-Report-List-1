<p><p>The Stereo Vision Sensor market comprises two main types: Monocular Stereo Vision Sensor and Binocular Stereo Vision Sensor. A monocular sensor uses a single camera to capture images and depth information, while a binocular sensor employs two cameras to mimic human vision and provide more accurate depth perception. Monocular sensors are more cost-effective but offer limited depth perception, whereas binocular sensors offer superior depth perception but are more expensive. Both types are used extensively in various applications like robotics, autonomous vehicles, and industrial automation to enable accurate object detection and navigation.</p></p>
