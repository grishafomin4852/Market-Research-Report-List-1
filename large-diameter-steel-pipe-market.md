<p><p>Large Diameter Steel Pipe Market Types consist of LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welding) Pipes, SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welding) Pipes, and Other markets. LSAW pipes are manufactured by bending and welding steel plates longitudinally, offering high strength and enhanced mechanical properties. SSAW pipes are created by forming a spiral shape from a hot-rolled steel strip and welding it using submerged arc welding. Both LSAW and SSAW pipes find applications in various industries including oil and gas, water supply, and construction. Additionally, the "Other" market category includes different types of large diameter steel pipes that cater to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
