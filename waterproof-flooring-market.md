<p><ul><li>Three Layer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterproof flooring market is divided into two main types: three layer and multilayer. Three-layer waterproof flooring is composed of a top layer, a vinyl or laminated layer, and a waterproof core layer. It offers good durability and water resistance. Multilayer waterproof flooring, on the other hand, has multiple layers with a rigid core. It provides enhanced stability, strength, and resistance against water damage. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in terms of durability, water resistance, and design options.</p></p>
