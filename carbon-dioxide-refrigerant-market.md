Secondly, the growing demand for energy-efficient refrigeration systems is fueling the market growth, as CO2 refrigerants have excellent thermodynamic properties and energy efficiency.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology and the development of innovative CO2 refrigeration systems are expected to drive market expansion. Additionally, the increasing awareness among consumers about the environmental impact of refrigerants is likely to create a demand for CO2 refrigerants in residential and commercial air conditioning.</p><p>In conclusion, the carbon dioxide refrigerant market is set for significant growth in the foreseeable future.
