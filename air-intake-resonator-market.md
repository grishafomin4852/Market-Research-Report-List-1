<p><strong>The Air Intake Resonator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Line Resonators</li><li>Side Branch Resonators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air intake resonator market consists of two main types: in-line resonators and side branch resonators. In-line resonators are placed within the air intake system, helping to reduce unwanted noise and vibrations created by the engine. On the other hand, side branch resonators are positioned at specific points along the air intake pipe to dampen and control air pressure pulses, resulting in smoother airflow and enhanced engine performance. These resonators play a crucial role in improving the overall efficiency and noise reduction of vehicles.</p></p>
