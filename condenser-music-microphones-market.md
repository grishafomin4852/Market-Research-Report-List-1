Small-diaphragm condenser microphones offer more detailed and accurate sound reproduction, making them popular for capturing acoustic instruments. The "others" category includes specialty condenser microphones designed for specific purposes, such as ribbon microphones for vintage sound or stereo microphones for immersive recording. Each type caters to different recording needs and preferences.</p></p>
