<p><p>The double-sided adhesive tape market comprises various types of tapes used for different applications. Thin transfer tapes are similar, but the adhesive is only on one side, typically used for attaching labels or graphics. Foam tapes have a foam layer between the adhesive, providing cushioning and vibration resistance. Fastening material tapes are used for secure and reliable bonding, while others encompass specialized tapes for specific applications.</p></p>
