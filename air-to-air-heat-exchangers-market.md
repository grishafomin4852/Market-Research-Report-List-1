Plate Type Air Heat Exchangers use flat plates with spaces in between to allow the air to flow through, promoting efficient heat transfer. Tube Type Air Heat Exchangers, on the other hand, use a network of tubes to exchange heat. Both types are widely used in industries, buildings, and HVAC systems to recover energy and improve indoor air quality.</p></p>
