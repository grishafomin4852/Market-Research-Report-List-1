<p><ul><li>Drum Brakes</li><li>Disc Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for brakes used in industrial machinery can be divided into two main types: drum brakes and disc brakes. Drum brakes are typically used in applications that require high torque and are suitable for heavy-duty machinery. They operate by using brake shoes that press against the inner surface of a rotating drum. Disc brakes, on the other hand, use brake pads to clamp down on a disc, providing a higher level of precision and control. They are commonly used in applications that require quick stopping or where space is limited.</p></p>
