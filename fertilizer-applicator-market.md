<p><p>The Fertilizer Applicator market consists of three main types: Mounted, Trailed, and Self-Propelled. Mounted Fertilizer Applicators are attached to tractors and spread fertilizer evenly on the ground. Trailed Fertilizer Applicators are towed behind tractors and evenly distribute fertilizer while moving. Self-Propelled Fertilizer Applicators are independent machines that move on their own and distribute fertilizer efficiently. These three types offer different levels of mobility and functionality, providing farmers with options to suit their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
