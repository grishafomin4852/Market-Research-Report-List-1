<p><p>Wellscreen and casing are integral components of oil and gas wells that provide stability and protection to the wellbore. Wellscreen is a perforated pipe that is placed inside the casing to allow oil or gas to flow into the well while preventing the entry of sand or other solids. Casing, on the other hand, is a larger diameter pipe that is cemented into the wellbore to provide structural support and prevent collapse.</p><p>The global market for wellscreens and casing is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
