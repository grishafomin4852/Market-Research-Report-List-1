<p><p>Din rail mount circuit breakers are a type of circuit protection devices that can be easily mounted on din rails in electrical panels. There are two main types of din rail mount circuit breakers: thermal and magnetic. Thermal circuit breakers use a bimetallic strip that reacts to heat caused by excessive current. On the other hand, magnetic circuit breakers operate based on the magnetic field generated by the current. Both types serve the purpose of protecting electrical systems from overloads and short circuits.</p></p>
