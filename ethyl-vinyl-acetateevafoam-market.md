<p><p>The Ethyl Vinyl Acetate (EVA) Foam market can be divided into three main types: extrusion molding, injection foaming molding, and compression molding. Extrusion molding involves the melting of EVA pellets and pushing the molten material through an extrusion die to form a continuous foam sheet. Injection foaming molding injects the molten EVA into a mold, where it expands and solidifies. Compression molding involves placing a predetermined amount of EVA material into a mold cavity and compressing it under heat and pressure until it takes the desired shape.</p></p>
