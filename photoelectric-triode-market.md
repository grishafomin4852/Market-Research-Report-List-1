<p><p>A photoelectric triode, also known as a phototriode, is a type of vacuum tube or electron tube that combines the functions of a phototransistor and a triode. It consists of a photocathode that emits electrons when exposed to light, an anode that collects these electrons, and a control grid that modulates the flow of electrons between the cathode and the anode.</p><p>The photoelectric triode market is experiencing steady growth due to its wide applications in various industries.
