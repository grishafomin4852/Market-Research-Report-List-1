<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Single Crystal Silicon Wafers market can be categorized into two types: CZ Method and FZ Method. CZ Method, also known as the Czochralski Method, involves melting high-purity polycrystalline silicon and re-solidifying it into a single crystal. This method is widely adopted due to its low cost and ease of production. FZ Method, on the other hand, utilizes a high-temperature heat source to melt and refine the silicon in a floating zone, resulting in a higher quality single crystal. This method is preferred for its superior purity and is commonly used in specialized applications.</p></p>
