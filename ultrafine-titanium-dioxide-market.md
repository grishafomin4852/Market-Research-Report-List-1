<p><p>The Ultrafine Titanium Dioxide market can be categorized into two types based on its composition: Anatase Based and Rutile Based. Anatase Based Titanium Dioxide is characterized by its small particle size and high surface area, making it suitable for applications requiring high reactivity and dispersibility. Rutile Based Titanium Dioxide, on the other hand, has a larger particle size and higher refractive index, making it more suitable for applications requiring high opacity and whiteness. These two types cater to different industrial needs and offer distinct properties for various applications.</p></p>
