<p><p>The automotive center stack display market consists of two main types: LED and OLED. LED (Light Emitting Diode) displays use an array of LEDs to generate light and produce images. They offer good brightness, reliability, and affordability, making them a popular choice for automotive displays. On the other hand, OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays utilize thin layers of organic compounds to emit light when an electric current is applied. They provide excellent color reproduction, contrast, and flexibility, which makes them suitable for curved or flexible surfaces in automotive displays.</p></p>
