<p><p>The Vacuum Valves Market consists of three main types: Low Vacuum Valves, High Vacuum Valves, and Ultra-high Vacuum Valves. Low Vacuum Valves are designed to regulate pressure in systems with pressure levels below atmospheric pressure. High Vacuum Valves are suitable for systems that require a higher degree of pressure control and can maintain a vacuum at relatively low pressure levels. Ultra-high Vacuum Valves are specifically developed for applications that demand extremely low pressure levels, offering the highest level of precision and control. All these valves play a crucial role in maintaining optimal vacuum levels in various industries and applications.</p></p>
