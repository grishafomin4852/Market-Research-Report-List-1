<p><p>Swage nipples are fittings used to connect pipes of different sizes. Concentric swage nipples have both ends of the same diameter, enabling a smooth transition from one pipe to another. On the other hand, eccentric swage nipples have one end with a smaller diameter than the other, allowing pipes to be connected without aligning perfectly. Concentric market in terms of swage nipples refers to the demand for fittings with equal diameters, while eccentric market refers to the demand for fittings with different diameters on each end.</p></p>
