<p><p>High alumina bricks are widely used in various industries due to their high heat resistance and excellent durability. They can be classified into two types, lining market and non-lining market. Lining market refers to the high alumina bricks that are used to line the furnaces, kilns, and other high-temperature equipment. These bricks form a protective layer that prevents the equipment from suffering damage caused by extreme heat. On the other hand, non-lining market refers to the high alumina bricks that are used in applications such as insulation, furnace plugging, and stack lining, where they provide thermal insulation and contribute to energy efficiency.</p></p>
