<p><p>The natural asphalt market can be categorized into two types: derived from coal and derived from oil. Coal-derived natural asphalt is obtained by processing coal, resulting in a harder and darker asphalt product. On the other hand, oil-derived natural asphalt is produced during the refining process of crude oil and tends to be softer and lighter in color. These two market types offer different characteristics and applications, making them suitable for various industries such as construction, paving, and waterproofing.</p></p>
