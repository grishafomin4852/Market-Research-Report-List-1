<p><p>ITO (Indium Tin Oxide) coated glass is a transparent conductive glass that is widely used in various electronic devices and applications. The integration of interactive and transparent display technologies in automobiles, such as heads-up display (HUD) and smart mirrors, is propelling the demand for ITO coated glass.
