<p><p>The Portable Spray Tan Machine market encompasses two main types: Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP) and High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP) machines. LVLP machines use a lower volume of air, resulting in a more controlled and precise spray tan application. On the other hand, HVLP machines use a higher volume of air to quickly cover larger areas with a spray tan. Both types offer portable solutions for achieving a flawless and even tan, but differ in the amount of air pressure used for application.</p></p>
