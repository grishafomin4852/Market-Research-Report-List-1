<p><p>The stainless steel flexible hose market consists of two main types: strip-wound hoses and corrugated hoses. Strip-wound hoses are made by wrapping a metal strip around a mandrel and then spirally winding it, resulting in a hose with excellent flexibility and strength. Corrugated hoses, on the other hand, are constructed with a series of ridges or corrugations, giving them increased flexibility and resistance to pressure. Both types have various applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
