<p><p>The Ring Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) market consists of two main types: LV MOV and HV-MV MOV. LV MOV refers to Low Voltage MOV, which is used in applications where the voltages are relatively low, typically below 1000 volts. On the other hand, HV-MV MOV stands for High Voltage-Medium Voltage MOV, which is specifically designed for higher voltage applications, typically above 1000 volts. Both types of MOVs are used to suppress and protect electrical devices and systems from transient voltage surges and spikes.</p></p>
