<p><p>Auto ventilated seats are a type of seating option equipped with built-in ventilation systems to improve comfort and air circulation in vehicles. Radial fans seats feature fans that blow air directly towards the occupant's body, providing a more targeted and efficient airflow. On the other hand, axial fans seats have fans that create a more widespread airflow across the entire seating surface. Both types aim to enhance thermal comfort and reduce sweating, making long drives more pleasant for passengers.</p></p>
