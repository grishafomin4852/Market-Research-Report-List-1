<p><p>The burn care centers market can be classified into two main types: in-hospital burn care centers and standalone burn care centers. In-hospital burn care centers are located within hospitals and provide specialized care for burn patients as part of the hospital's overall services. These centers are integrated into the hospital setting and benefit from the resources and infrastructure of the hospital. On the other hand, standalone burn care centers operate independently from hospitals and solely focus on providing specialized burn care services. They have their own dedicated facilities, staff, and equipment solely dedicated to burn care.</p></p>
