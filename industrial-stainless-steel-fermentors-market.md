<p><p>Industrial stainless steel fermentors are used in various industries for the production of biotechnology and pharmaceutical products. There are three main types of fermentors: batch fermenters, fed-batch fermenters, and continuous fermenters. Batch fermenters are used for small-scale production, where all ingredients are added at once and the process is allowed to run its course. Fed-batch fermenters involve the continuous addition of ingredients to improve productivity. Continuous fermenters allow for a constant flow of ingredients and removal of the final product, enabling large-scale and continuous production.</p></p>
