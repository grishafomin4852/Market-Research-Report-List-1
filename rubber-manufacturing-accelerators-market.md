<p><p>Rubber Manufacturing Accelerators are substances used in the rubber manufacturing process to speed up vulcanization and enhance the performance of rubber products. Different types of accelerators are used in this market, including MBT, MBTS, CBS, TBBS, and others. MBT (Mercaptobenzothiazole) is a widely used accelerator for natural and synthetic rubber, while MBTS (Dibenzothiazyl disulfide) is mainly employed for curing high-temperature vulcanized rubbers. CBS (Cyclohexylbenzothiazole sulfenamide) is typically used as a primary accelerator, and TBBS (N-tert-butyl-2-benzothiazolesulfenamide) is utilized to accelerate vulcanization in synthetic rubbers. Other accelerators from the "others" category are also employed based on specific rubber manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
