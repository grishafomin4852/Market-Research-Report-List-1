<p><p>The rubber accelerator market is categorized into several types, including MBT (2-Mercaptobenzothiazole), MBTS (Dibenzothiazyl Disulfide), CBS (Cz) N-Cyclohexyl-2-benzothiazole Sulfenamide), and TBBS (N-tert-Butyl-2-benzothiazolesulfenamide). MBT accelerators are widely used in the rubber industry due to their ability to improve mechanical properties and aging resistance. MBTS accelerators exhibit better storage stability, while CBS accelerators enhance the vulcanization rate of rubber. TBBS accelerators offer excellent scorch resistance and are suitable for manufacturing radial tires. These accelerators play a crucial role in improving the performance of rubber products in various applications.</p></p>
