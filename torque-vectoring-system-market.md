<p><p>Torque Vectoring System Market Types - Front Wheel Drive (FWD), Rear Wheel Drive (RWD), All Wheel Drive/Four Wheel Drive (4WD) - are different automotive drivetrain configurations. FWD sends power to the front wheels, offering better fuel efficiency but lacking traction control. RWD transfers power to the rear wheels, providing better acceleration and handling but less control in slippery conditions. AWD/4WD distributes torque to all four wheels, improving traction and stability, especially in off-road scenarios. Each type has its advantages and limitations, catering to different user preferences and driving conditions.</p></p>
