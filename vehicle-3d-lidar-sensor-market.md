<p><p>The vehicle 3D LiDAR sensor market consists of two types: mechanical LiDAR and solid-state LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR sensors utilize moving parts to scan the surroundings and measure distance. They offer high-resolution and long-range capabilities but are bulky and expensive. Solid-state LiDAR sensors, on the other hand, use stationary components like laser diodes and photodetectors to emit and receive light pulses. They are smaller, more affordable, and consume less power. Solid-state LiDAR sensors are becoming increasingly popular due to their compact design and improved reliability.</p></p>
