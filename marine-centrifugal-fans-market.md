<p><strong>The Marine Centrifugal Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Drive</li><li>Belt Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine centrifugal fans are used in marine applications for ventilation and air circulation. The market for these fans is categorized into two types: direct drive and belt drive. Direct drive fans have the motor directly connected to the impeller, providing efficient and low-maintenance operation. On the other hand, belt drive fans have the motor connected to the impeller through a belt and pulley system, allowing for flexibility in speed adjustment. Both types offer reliable performance in marine environments, with direct drive fans being preferred for their simplicity and lower overall cost.</p></p>
