<p><p>The offshore drilling riser market can be classified into two main types: marine drilling riser and tie-back drilling riser. Marine drilling riser refers to the riser system used in deepwater drilling operations, typically consisting of a series of connected pipes that facilitate the transfer of drilling fluids between the drilling vessel and the subsea well. On the other hand, tie-back drilling riser is used for connecting existing platform infrastructure with additional wells to maximize production. It allows for efficient drilling and completion operations by utilizing existing facilities.</p></p>
