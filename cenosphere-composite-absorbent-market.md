Metal cenosphere composite absorbents utilize hollow metal microspheres, offering excellent thermal conductivity and stability. Polymer cenosphere composite absorbents consist of hollow polymer microspheres, providing good thermal insulation and chemical resistance. These different types cater to diverse industrial applications that require specific absorbency properties.</p></p>
