<p><p>The metalworking lathes market can be categorized into three main types: center lathe, engine lathe, and bench lathe. Center lathes are versatile machines used for general purpose turning operations on various materials. Engine lathes are larger and more powerful, mainly used for heavy-duty industrial applications like metal shaping and threading. Bench lathes, on the other hand, are smaller and portable, often used for light-duty tasks, repairs, and hobbyist projects. Each type serves different purposes within the metalworking industry, catering to different levels of precision, power, and portability requirements.</p></p>
