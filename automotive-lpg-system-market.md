<p><p>The Automotive LPG System market encompasses various types of systems. Single Point (or Mixer) Open Loop Type Systems are designed to mix LPG with air before entering the engine's combustion chamber, offering a simple and cost-effective solution. Single Point (or Mixer) Closed Loop Type Systems, on the other hand, use an oxygen sensor to measure the air-fuel ratio and adjust the LPG flow accordingly, ensuring optimal performance and emission control. Apart from these, the market also includes other types of systems that employ different technologies and components to enable the efficient utilization of LPG in automotive engines.</p></p>
