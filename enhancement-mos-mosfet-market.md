<p><p>The Enhancement MOS MOSFET market consists of two main types: P-channel type MOS and N-channel type MOS. P-channel type MOSFETs are designed with a positively charged channel, allowing for conduction when a negative voltage is applied to the gate terminal. N-Channel type MOSFETs, on the other hand, have a negatively charged channel and conduct when a positive gate voltage is applied. Both types find applications in various electronic devices, such as amplifiers, power supplies, and switches, due to their ability to control the flow of current effectively.</p></p>
