<p><p>Drum cutters for excavators are powerful attachments used in construction and mining industries to cut through hard materials like rock and concrete. There are two types of drum cutters: transverse and axial. Transverse drum cutters have a rotating drum that cuts in a side-to-side motion, making them ideal for trenching and profiling applications. Axial drum cutters, on the other hand, have a drum that cuts in a rotating motion, making them suitable for continuous excavating and material removal tasks. Each type of drum cutter serves specific purposes based on the desired operation and workload.</p></p>
