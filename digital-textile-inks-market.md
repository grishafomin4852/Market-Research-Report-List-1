<p><p>Digital textile inks market types include dispersed inks, reactive inks, acid inks, and pigment inks. Dispersed inks are finely dispersed into small particles and are suitable for synthetic fibers. Reactive inks bond with the fabric through chemical reactions, offering excellent color fastness. Acid inks are used for protein fibers and provide vibrant colors. Pigment inks contain solid, insoluble color particles and are versatile for various fabric types. These different types of inks cater to specific fabric needs, ensuring high-quality and vibrant digital textile prints.</p></p>
