<p><p>The Disc Tube Reverse Osmosis (DTRO) and Disc Tube Nano-Filtration (DTNF) membrane technologies are used in water treatment. DTRO membranes provide a high level of salt rejection and have applications in desalination. DTNF membranes have a narrower pore size and are suitable for removing smaller particles and organic matter. Both technologies are used to purify water by removing impurities through a semi-permeable membrane. They find applications in industries such as water and wastewater treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
