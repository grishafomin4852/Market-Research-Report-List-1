<p><p>Low-Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC) coating additives are substances used in paint and coating formulations to reduce the emission of harmful VOCs. There are several types of these additives available in the market. </p><p>1. Rheology Modifier: These additives improve the flow and consistency of the coating, ensuring smooth application and uniform film thickness.</p><p>2. Defoamer: Defoamers prevent the formation of foam during the mixing and application of coatings, enhancing their appearance and performance.</p><p>3. Wetting Agent: Wetting agents reduce the surface tension of the coating, allowing it to spread evenly and adhere better to the substrate.</p><p>4. Dispersant: Dispersants help in the dispersion of pigment particles, preventing their agglomeration and improving color development.</p><p>5. Other: This category includes various additional additives such as leveling agents, anti-settling agents, and anti-skinning agents that provide specific functionalities to the coating formulation.</p></p>
