<p><p>Man-made fiber, also known as synthetic fiber, is a type of fiber that is synthesized through chemical processes rather than being naturally derived from plants or animals. These fibers are widely used in industries such as textiles, automotive, construction, healthcare, and packaging.</p><p>One of the key trends in the man-made fiber market is the increasing demand for eco-friendly and sustainable fibers. Consumers are becoming more conscious of the environmental impact of synthetic fibers, leading to a growing interest in green alternatives. This has led to the development of fibers made from recycled materials, bio-based sources, and biodegradable polymers.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the rising demand for high-performance fibers, particularly in the automotive and aerospace industries.
