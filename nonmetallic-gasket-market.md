<p><p>The nonmetallic gasket market is comprised of three main types: elastomeric gaskets, advanced fiber-based gaskets, and plastic-based gaskets. Elastomeric gaskets, made from rubber or synthetic rubber materials, are flexible and seal well under low pressure conditions. Advanced fiber-based gaskets, produced from materials like aramid, carbon, or fiberglass, offer high performance and are suitable for extreme temperatures and pressures. Plastic-based gaskets, made from materials like PTFE or PVC, are chemically resistant and often used in industries where corrosion is a concern. These gasket types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
