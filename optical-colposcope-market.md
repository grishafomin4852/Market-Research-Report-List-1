<p><p>Optical colposcopes are medical devices used for examining the cervix. They are available in two types: monocular and binocular. Monocular colposcopes have a single eyepiece, providing a single perspective of the cervix. On the other hand, binocular colposcopes have two eyepieces, offering a more immersive and three-dimensional view. The choice between these types depends on the preferences and requirements of the healthcare professional or the medical facility. Both types, however, serve the same purpose of aiding in the diagnosis and treatment of cervical abnormalities.</p></p>
