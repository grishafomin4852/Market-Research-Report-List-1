<p><p>The Electric Yacht Charter market can be categorized into two main types: Pure Electric and Electric Hybrid. </p><p>Pure Electric charters exclusively rely on electric propulsion systems, utilizing battery power to propel the yacht. This type of charter is mainly eco-friendly, emitting zero emissions and reducing noise pollution. </p><p>On the other hand, Electric Hybrid charters combine electric propulsion with a secondary power source, typically a diesel or gasoline generator. This hybrid system ensures enhanced range and flexibility, as the secondary power source can be used when required. It also provides the advantage of reduced fuel consumption and lower emissions compared to traditional yachts.</p></p>
