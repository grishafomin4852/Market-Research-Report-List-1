<p><p>TCR Static Var Compensator (SVC) is a device used in electric power systems to regulate voltage and reactive power. It is a type of static compensator that consists of thyristor-controlled reactors (TCR) and fixed capacitors. The TCR controls the reactive power flow whereas the fixed capacitors provide the required reactive power support.</p><p>The TCR Static Var Compensator (SVC) market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for reliable and efficient power transmission is a major driving factor for this market. SVCs help in maintaining voltage stability and reducing transmission losses, thereby improving the overall power system performance. The growing investments in grid infrastructure development and the shift towards renewable energy sources are also driving the demand for SVCs.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing industrialization and urbanization in developing countries are creating a need for improved power quality and reliability, which further fuels the market growth.
