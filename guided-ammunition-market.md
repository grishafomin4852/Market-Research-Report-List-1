<p><p>Guided ammunition refers to advanced munitions that have the capability to change course or use precision guidance systems to ensure accurate targeting. Different types of guided ammunition exist, including laser-guided, radar-guided, infrared-guided, GPS-guided, and others. Laser-guided ammunition uses laser beams for precise targeting, while radar-guided ammunition uses radar systems to track and guide projectiles. Infrared-guided ammunition relies on heat-seeking sensors to locate and engage targets. GPS-guided ammunition utilizes satellite navigation systems for accurate positioning and targeting. Lastly, other types of guided ammunition encompass a range of technologies such as radio frequency guidance systems and image recognition techniques.</p></p>
