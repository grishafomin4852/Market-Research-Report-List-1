<p><strong>The Building Asphalt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer-Modified Bitumen</li><li>Bitumen Emulsion</li><li>Cutback Emulsion</li><li>Oxidized Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building asphalt market includes various types of asphalt products used in construction. Polymer-modified bitumen is a type of asphalt mixed with polymers to enhance its properties, such as elasticity and durability. Bitumen emulsion is a mixture of asphalt, water, and emulsifying agents, used for surface treatments. Cutback emulsion is bitumen mixed with solvents for easier application in cold temperatures. Oxidized emulsion is a type of asphalt modified through a chemical process to increase hardness and resistance to weathering. These different asphalt types cater to different construction requirements and conditions.</p></p>
