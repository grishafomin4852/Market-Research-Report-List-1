There are two types of frameless glass partitions: single glass partitions and double glass partitions. Single glass partitions consist of a single layer of glass, while double glass partitions have two layers of glass with an air or acoustic gap in between. Both types offer acoustic privacy and flexibility in terms of design and installation options.</p></p>
