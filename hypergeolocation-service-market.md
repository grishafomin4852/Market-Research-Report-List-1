<p><p>Hypergeolocation service is the process of determining the precise location of an object or device using advanced technology. There are three main market types in this field. PPP (Precise Point Positioning) is a service that provides accurate positioning using satellite data. RTK (Real-Time Kinematic) is a market that offers real-time positioning by transmitting and receiving signals from a base station. RTK-PPP combines the benefits of both PPP and RTK, resulting in highly accurate and real-time positioning services. These market types cater to various industries, such as navigation, transportation, and surveying.</p></p>
