<p><p>The AI interactive electronic whiteboards market consists of two types: front projection and rear projection. Front projection whiteboards use a projector placed in front of the board to display images and interactive content. This type is more commonly used and offers high brightness and image quality. On the other hand, rear projection whiteboards have the projector behind the board, resulting in a shadow-free display and less chance of glare. Both types provide interactive features, such as touch-sensitive surfaces and AI integration, enabling enhanced collaboration and presentation capabilities in various settings.</p></p>
