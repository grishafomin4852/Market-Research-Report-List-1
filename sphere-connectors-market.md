<p><strong>The Sphere Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Sphere Connector</li><li>Twin Sphere Connector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sphere Connectors market consists of two main types of connectors: Single Sphere Connector and Twin Sphere Connector. A Single Sphere Connector is a flexible joint that allows movement in one direction, providing vibration and noise reduction. It is commonly used in plumbing, HVAC systems, and exhaust systems. On the other hand, a Twin Sphere Connector is designed to provide movement in two directions, offering even higher flexibility and absorption of axial, lateral, and angular movements. This makes it suitable for applications involving high levels of vibration, such as power plants and large industrial machinery.</p></p>
