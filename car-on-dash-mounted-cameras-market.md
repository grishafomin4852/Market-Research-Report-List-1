<p><p>Car on-dash mounted cameras are becoming increasingly popular for recording activities on the road. There are two main types: single-channel and dual-channel cameras. Single-channel cameras capture footage from a single perspective, usually facing forward, and are suitable for basic recording needs. Dual-channel cameras, on the other hand, have two separate lenses and record both the front and rear view of the vehicle simultaneously. This type provides a more comprehensive view of the surroundings and is ideal for those seeking enhanced security and evidence in case of accidents or incidents.</p></p>
