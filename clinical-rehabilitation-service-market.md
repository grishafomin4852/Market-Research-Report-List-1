<p><p>Clinical rehabilitation services refer to a range of interventions and therapies designed to help individuals recover and maintain their physical, cognitive, and psychological health after experiencing a disabling condition or injury. As the global population continues to age, the incidence of chronic diseases and disabilities is expected to rise significantly. This, coupled with advancements in medical technology and an increasing focus on patient-centric care, is driving the demand for clinical rehabilitation services. Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has highlighted the importance of rehabilitation services in supporting patients recovering from the virus or its long-term effects.</p><p>Additionally, the market is witnessing a shift towards outpatient rehabilitation services due to increasing healthcare costs and a preference for home-based care. Technological advancements, such as telehealth and remote monitoring, are also playing a significant role in expanding access to rehabilitation services and improving patient outcomes.</p><p>However, the clinical rehabilitation service market is not without its challenges. Limited insurance coverage, especially in developing countries, can hinder the growth of the market.
