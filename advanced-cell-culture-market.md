<p><p>The Advanced Cell Culture market consists of two main types: 2D Cell Culture and 3D Cell Culture. In 2D Cell Culture, cells are grown and cultured on a flat, two-dimensional surface like a petri dish. This method is simpler and less expensive but may not accurately represent the complexity of human tissues. 3D Cell Culture, on the other hand, involves creating a three-dimensional environment that mimics the conditions found in the human body more closely. This method allows for better cell growth and interaction, making it more suitable for studying diseases and developing new drugs.</p></p>
