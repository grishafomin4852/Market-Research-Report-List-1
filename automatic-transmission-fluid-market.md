<p><p>The Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF) market consists of two types: Semi-Synthetic and Full-Synthetic ATF. Semi-Synthetic ATF is a blend of mineral and synthetic base oils, providing improved performance and protection compared to traditional mineral ATF. On the other hand, Full-Synthetic ATF is composed entirely of synthetic base oils, offering even higher performance and protection levels. Both types are designed to lubricate and cool automatic transmissions, ensuring smooth shifting, reducing wear, and extending the lifespan of the transmission system.</p></p>
