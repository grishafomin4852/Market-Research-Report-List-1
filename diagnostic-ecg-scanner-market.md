<p><strong>The Diagnostic ECG Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analogue</li><li>Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diagnostic ECG Scanner market is divided into two types: Analogue and Digital. Analogue ECG scanners use traditional methods that involve recording electrical signals from the heart using an analog signal. These scanners provide a real-time visual representation of the heart's electrical activity. On the other hand, digital ECG scanners use advanced technology to convert the electrical signals into digital data, which can be processed and analyzed by computers. These scanners offer higher accuracy, convenience, and can store data electronically for future reference.</p></p>
