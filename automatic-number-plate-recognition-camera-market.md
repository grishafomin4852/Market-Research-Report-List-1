<p><p>Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) camera, also known as License Plate Recognition (LPR) camera, is a technology that uses optical character recognition on images to read vehicle license plates. It captures and analyzes license plate information and converts it into a digital format for further processing, such as vehicle tracking, access control, law enforcement, and parking management.</p><p>ANPR cameras consist of a high-resolution camera, optical character recognition software, and image processing capabilities. They can accurately capture license plate information even at high speeds and in challenging conditions, such as low light or adverse weather. The captured data is usually compared against a database for identification or verification purposes.</p><p>The global ANPR camera market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
