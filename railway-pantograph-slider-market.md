<p><p>A railway pantograph slider is a mechanism used in electric railway systems to transfer power from overhead lines to the train. These pantographs collect electrical energy, which is then transferred to the train's electric motors for propulsion.</p><p>The Railway Pantograph Slider Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
