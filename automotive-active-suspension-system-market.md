<p><p>The automotive active suspension system market consists of various types of suspension systems, including air suspension, hydraulic suspension, electromagnetic suspension, and electro-hydraulic suspension. Air suspension utilizes compressed air to maintain desired vehicle height and improve ride quality. Hydraulic suspension utilizes hydraulic fluid to provide better control and damping of vehicle movements. Electromagnetic suspension uses electromagnetic technology to adjust suspension stiffness according to road conditions. Electro-hydraulic suspension combines the benefits of hydraulic and electromagnetic suspension systems, offering enhanced stability and comfort while ensuring optimal vehicle performance.</p></p>
