<p><ul><li>PSA</li><li>CMS</li><li>Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Nitrogen Generator market consists of three main types - PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption), CMS (Carbon Molecular Sieve), and Membrane. PSA technology utilizes adsorbents to separate nitrogen from other gases. CMS technology uses carbon molecular sieves to selectively separate nitrogen. Membrane technology employs a permeable membrane to filter out nitrogen from other gases. These types of Medical Nitrogen Generators are widely used in hospitals, clinics, and laboratories for various medical applications, including surgical procedures, equipment sterilization, drug preservation, and cryotherapy.</p></p>
