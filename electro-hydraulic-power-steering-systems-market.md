<p><strong>The Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EHPS</li><li>P-EHPS</li><li>R-EHPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering Systems (EHPS) market consists of three main types - C-EHPS, P-EHPS, and R-EHPS. C-EHPS (Column-EHPS) is a system where the electric motor is attached to the steering column. P-EHPS (Pinion-EHPS) has the electric motor connected to the steering gear pinion. Lastly, R-EHPS (Rack-EHPS) has the electric motor linked to the rack gear. These systems combine hydraulic power assistance with electric control, enhancing steering performance and efficient energy usage. Each type offers unique features and advantages, catering to diverse applications and requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
