<p><p>Thermal Barrier Coatings (TBC) can be categorized into two main types: metal/alloy coatings and ceramic coatings. Metal/alloy coatings are typically applied on metallic surfaces to protect them from high temperatures. They are known for their ability to resist oxidation and provide good thermal insulation. Ceramic coatings, on the other hand, are used primarily in non-metallic materials, such as ceramics or composites. These coatings offer excellent thermal insulation properties, as well as high resistance to wear, corrosion, and oxidation. Both types of TBCs find applications in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, energy, and more.</p></p>
