<p><p>An automatic gearbox, also known as an automatic transmission, is a type of gearbox that changes gears automatically in response to speed and load changes without the need for driver intervention. Unlike a manual gearbox, which requires the driver to shift gears manually using a clutch pedal and gear lever, an automatic gearbox shifts gears automatically using a hydraulic system, electronic sensors, and a torque converter.</p><p>Market analysis suggests that the global automatic gearbox market is poised for significant growth in the coming years. The market is driven by various factors, such as increased demand for passenger and commercial vehicles with automatic transmissions, technological advancements in transmission systems, and the rising adoption of electric vehicles. On the other hand, CVTs offer seamless gear ratio changes, resulting in a smoother driving experience and improved fuel economy.</p><p>The integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies in automatic gearboxes is another emerging trend.
