<p><p>The Timber Wrap Films market consists of three main types: HDPE Wrap Films, LDPE Wrap Films, and PP Wrap Films. HDPE Wrap Films, made from high-density polyethylene, are known for their strength and durability, making them ideal for use in the timber industry. LDPE Wrap Films, made from low-density polyethylene, offer excellent flexibility and are commonly used for wrapping and protecting timber during transportation. PP Wrap Films, made from polypropylene, provide both strength and clarity, making them suitable for wrapping timber in retail settings where visibility is important.</p></p>
