Solvent-based spray adhesive is composed of organic solvents, providing strong bonding power but emitting harmful fumes. Water-based spray adhesive offers a safer alternative, as it is free of toxic substances but with slightly weaker adhesion. Hot melt spray adhesive contains thermoplastic substances that are heated to create a molten adhesive, providing quick bonding but typically used in industrial applications. Each market type caters to different needs, offering a range of options based on bonding strength, safety, and application requirements.</p></p>
