<p><p>Robotic-assisted hip replacement market types include passive systems, semi-active systems, and active systems. Semi-active systems offer a combination of surgeon control and robotic assistance, allowing for greater precision. Active systems provide the highest level of robotic assistance, allowing the robot to perform certain tasks independently. These systems enhance surgical accuracy, reduce complications, and improve patient outcomes for hip replacement surgeries.</p></p>
