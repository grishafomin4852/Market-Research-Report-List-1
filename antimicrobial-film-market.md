<p><p>The antimicrobial film market involves two main types of films: polyester film and PVC film. Polyester film is a type of plastic film made from polyethylene terephthalate. It is widely used in applications such as packaging, electronics, and imaging due to its excellent mechanical properties and low permeability to gases and moisture. PVC film, on the other hand, is a versatile type of plastic film made from polyvinyl chloride. It is commonly used in applications such as medical devices, food packaging, and building materials due to its durability and resistance to chemicals. Both polyester film and PVC film can be treated with antimicrobial agents to prevent the growth of microorganisms and provide additional protection against bacteria, viruses, and fungi.</p></p>
