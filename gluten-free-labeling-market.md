<p><p>Gluten-free labeling market includes different types such as Black Label, Green Label, and Other market. Green Label indicates products that are self-declared gluten-free by the manufacturer without third-party verification. Consumers should exercise caution with these products as they may not always meet the same quality standards. The Other market refers to products that may contain gluten but are labeled as gluten-free due to lack of standardized regulations or misleading claims. This market requires consumer diligence to ensure safe consumption.</p></p>
