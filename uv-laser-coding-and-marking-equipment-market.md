<p><p>UV laser coding and marking equipment is used to create permanent marks or codes on various materials. In mask marking, a stencil is used to block certain areas of the material, allowing the laser to mark the desired pattern. Array marking involves using an array of individual laser beams to mark multiple points simultaneously. Scanning marking uses a moving laser beam to rapidly mark a continuous pattern. All these types of UV laser coding and marking equipment offer high precision and speed, making them popular in industries like electronics, automotive, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
