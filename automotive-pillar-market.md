Pillar A refers to the front windshield pillar, which is usually thicker and stronger to support the weight of the roof. Pillar B separates the front and rear windows, providing structural stability to the vehicle. Pillar C is located behind the rear window, ensuring the overall rigidity of the car. Lastly, Pillar D connects the rear windshield to the trunk, contributing to the vehicle's structural integrity and safety.</p></p>
