<p><p>Roofing membranes are essential materials used in the construction industry for waterproofing and protecting roofs. There are various types of roofing membranes available. Thermoplastic membranes are made of plastic polymers that can be repeatedly softened by heat and rehardened upon cooling. Synthetic rubber membranes, also known as thermoset membranes, are made from rubber-like materials that become permanently solid after curing. Modified bitumen membranes are made by adding polymers to bitumen, making them more flexible and durable. Each type offers specific benefits and is chosen based on the project's requirements.</p></p>
