<p><ul><li>Radial Tyre</li><li>Bias Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle tyre market can be categorized into two types: Radial Tyre and Bias Tyre. Radial Tyres are designed with cords laid at a 90-degree angle to the tread centerline, allowing for better traction, heat dissipation, and fuel efficiency. They provide a smooth and comfortable ride. On the other hand, Bias Tyres are constructed with cords positioned at an angle to the tread centerline, resulting in more rigid sidewalls and increased load carrying capacity. Bias Tyres are suitable for heavy-duty applications and rough terrains due to their strong construction.</p></p>
