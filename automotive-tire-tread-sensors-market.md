<p><p>The automotive tire tread sensors market comprises two types: Tire Integrated Sensor (TIS) and Tire Tread Sensor (TTS). TIS is a sensor embedded within the tire, allowing real-time measurement of tire pressure, temperature, and depth. This data helps enhance vehicle safety and fuel efficiency. On the other hand, TTS is a sensor located on the tire's surface, measuring the tread depth. By providing this information, TTS enables drivers to monitor tire condition and tread wear, promoting better maintenance and preventing accidents caused by insufficient tread depth.</p></p>
