<p><p>Instant adhesives, also known as super glues or cyanoacrylate adhesives, are a type of adhesive that bond quickly and strongly to various materials. Cyanoacrylate instant adhesives are based on a fast-acting cyanoacrylate resin, which reacts with moisture in the air to form a tough bond. On the other hand, epoxy-based instant adhesives utilize epoxy resin for bonding, offering excellent resistance to temperature, chemicals, and impact. Both types of instant adhesives are widely used in industries such as automotive, electronics, medical, and construction for their rapid bonding capabilities and strong adhesion to diverse substrates.</p></p>
