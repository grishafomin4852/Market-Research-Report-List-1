<p><p>Photovoltaics market is divided into two major types: organic and inorganic. Organic photovoltaics (OPV) utilize organic materials, such as polymers or small molecules, to convert sunlight into electricity. OPV offers advantages like flexibility, light weight, and low-cost production. On the other hand, inorganic photovoltaics use inorganic materials, such as silicon, to convert sunlight into electricity. Inorganic photovoltaics, particularly crystalline silicon, are widely used due to their higher efficiency and longer lifespan. Each market type has its own unique characteristics, making it suitable for different applications and market segments.</p></p>
