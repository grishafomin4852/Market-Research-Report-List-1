<p><p>Hydro lubricants market comprises two main types: water-based and oil-based lubricants. Water-based lubricants are formulated by adding water to a lubricating agent, creating a thin film that reduces friction between moving surfaces. They are often used for applications where avoiding oil contamination is crucial, such as in the food, pharmaceutical, and medical industries. On the other hand, oil-based lubricants are formulated by blending lubricating oils with additives. They offer excellent lubrication and are widely used in automotive, manufacturing, and heavy machinery industries.</p></p>
