There are two main market types: High Methoxyl (HM) pectin and Low Methoxyl (LM) pectin. HM pectin requires the presence of sugar and high acidity to form a gel, while LM pectin requires calcium ions and low acidity. HM pectin is commonly used in regular sugar preserves, while LM pectin is preferred for low sugar or sugar-free products.</p></p>
