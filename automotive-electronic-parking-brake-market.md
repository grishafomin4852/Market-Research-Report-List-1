<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Parking Brake (EPB) market consists of three types: FMVSS 105, FMVSS 135, and ECE 13H. FMVSS 105 refers to the Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard 105, which specifies the requirements for EPB performance in vehicles sold in the United States. FMVSS 135 is another safety standard from the US Department of Transportation, focusing on EPB requirements for passenger cars. ECE 13H, on the other hand, is a regulation by the United Nations Economic Commission for Europe, setting EPB performance standards for European vehicles.</p></p>
