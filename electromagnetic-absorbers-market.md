<p><p>Electromagnetic absorbers are materials designed to absorb and dampen electromagnetic waves. There are three main types of electromagnetic absorbers in the market: polypropylene absorbers, ferrite absorbers, and others. Polypropylene absorbers are made of polypropylene foam and are known for their excellent absorption properties across a wide frequency range. Ferrite absorbers consist of ferrite particles embedded in a matrix material, offering high absorption at lower frequencies. The "others" category includes various absorber materials like carbon-based absorbers, conductive polymers, and hybrid absorbers, each with unique absorption characteristics.</p></p>
