<p><p>The AI-embedded Digital Instrument Cluster market comprises three types: LCD, OLED, and TFT-LCD. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) technology uses liquid crystals to create images and requires a backlight to illuminate the screen. OLED (Organic Light-Emitting Diode) technology emits light when an electric current passes through organic materials, enabling thinner and more flexible displays. TFT-LCD (Thin Film Transistor-Liquid Crystal Display) combines LCD and TFT technologies, offering high-resolution images with better color accuracy and wider viewing angles. These display technologies are utilized in digital instrument clusters integrating AI capabilities for enhanced user experience and advanced vehicle information display.</p></p>
