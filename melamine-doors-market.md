<p><p>Melamine doors are available in two types: MDF (Medium-Density Fiberboard) core and Particleboard core. MDF core melamine doors are made from an engineered wood product that is dense, strong, and free from knots. It provides a smooth surface for applying the melamine finish, making it ideal for modern and sleek designs. On the other hand, Particleboard core melamine doors are made from wood particles and are often more affordable than MDF core doors. While less durable, they still offer a smooth and even surface for the melamine finish.</p></p>
