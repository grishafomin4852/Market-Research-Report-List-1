<p><p>Vertical Pressure Leaf Filters (VPLF) are filtration systems used in various industries. The market types for VPLFs can be categorized as Dry Cake Discharge and Wet Cake Discharge. In the Dry Cake Discharge market, the filter removes solid particles from the liquid, resulting in a dry cake formation. This type of filter is commonly used when the solid particles have high value or need to be reused. In the Wet Cake Discharge market, the filter separates the solid particles from the liquid, but the cake formed is wet and requires further processing or disposal. This type of filter is typically used when the solid particles have low value or need to be discarded.</p></p>
