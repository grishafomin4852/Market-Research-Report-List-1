<p><p>Laser systems are utilized in various industries for different applications. The market for laser systems can be categorized into four types. Gas lasers employ gas mixture to generate laser beams and are commonly used in medical and industrial sectors. Optical fiber lasers use a fiber as the gain medium, providing higher efficiency and flexibility. Solid-state lasers use solid materials like crystals to produce laser beams and are widely used in manufacturing and scientific research. Lastly, other laser systems include semiconductor lasers, excimer lasers, and dye lasers, each with unique characteristics catering to specific industries and purposes.</p></p>
