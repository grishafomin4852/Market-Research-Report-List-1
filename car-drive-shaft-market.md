<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The car drive shaft market consists of two main types: half shafts and propeller shafts. Half shafts are used in front-wheel drive vehicles and connect the transmission to the front wheels, allowing for power transfer. They consist of two separate shafts with constant velocity joints at each end. On the other hand, propeller shafts are used in rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive vehicles. They transmit power from the transmission to the rear wheels, ensuring smooth rotation. Propeller shafts are a single, longer shaft with center bearings for support.</p></p>
