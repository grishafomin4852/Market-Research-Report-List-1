<p><p>The empty can inspection system market is categorized into different types of inspection methods. Optical inspection utilizes cameras and sensors to identify defects or abnormalities on the surface of empty cans. Ultrasonic testing involves the use of high-frequency sound waves to detect internal defects such as leaks or cracks. X-ray inspection utilizes X-rays to examine the internal structure and identify defects that are not visible on the surface. The "Others" category includes additional inspection methods like magnetic particle testing or eddy current testing that are less commonly used in the industry.</p></p>
