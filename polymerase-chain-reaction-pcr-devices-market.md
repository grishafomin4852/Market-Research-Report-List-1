<p><p>The Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) devices market consists of two types: Digital PCR and Real-time PCR. Digital PCR divides a DNA sample into many small partitions and then amplifies each partition to determine the exact amount of DNA present. On the other hand, Real-time PCR allows the amplification of DNA to be monitored in real-time using fluorescent dyes. Both types of PCR devices play a crucial role in various applications such as genotyping, molecular diagnostics, and gene expression analysis.</p></p>
