<p><p>General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS) is a versatile plastic material used in various industries. The GPPS market can be categorized into three types: extrusion molding, injection molding, and others. </p><p>Extrusion molding involves melting GPPS pellets and pushing them through a die to form continuous shapes like sheets, tubes, and profiles. Injection molding, on the other hand, refers to the process of injecting molten GPPS into a mold cavity, allowing it to solidify and form complex 3D shapes like bottles, containers, and automotive parts. The "others" category includes alternative methods like blow molding and thermoforming, which are used to create customized GPPS products for specific applications.</p></p>
