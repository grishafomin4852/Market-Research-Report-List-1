<p><p>The clinical robotic systems market consists of two main types: robotic arms and track robots. Robotic arms are highly advanced machines designed to perform precise surgical procedures with the help of specific tools. They are controlled by surgeons who operate them remotely. On the other hand, track robots are mobile systems that move along tracks in operating rooms. They are used to deliver medical instruments and supplies to surgeons during procedures. Both types of systems play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of surgical outcomes in clinical settings.</p></p>
