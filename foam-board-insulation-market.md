<p><p>Foam board insulation is categorized into three types: EPS foam board, XPS foam board, and polyurethane foam board. EPS foam board, also known as expanded polystyrene, is lightweight, affordable, and offers good insulation properties. XPS foam board, or extruded polystyrene, is denser and more rigid, providing higher resistance to moisture. Polyurethane foam board is a versatile and efficient insulation material that offers both thermal and acoustic insulation. These types of foam boards are widely used in construction and building applications to enhance energy efficiency and reduce heat loss.</p></p>
