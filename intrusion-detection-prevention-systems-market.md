<p><p>Intrusion Detection and Prevention Systems (IDPS) are security solutions used to identify and defend against unauthorized activities in computer networks. Hardware-based IDPS typically consist of physical devices, such as firewalls and network sensors, that monitor network traffic and detect malicious activities. Software-based IDPS, on the other hand, are applications installed on servers or endpoints that analyze network data and generate alerts or take automated actions to prevent intrusions.
