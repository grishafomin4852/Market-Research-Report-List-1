Firstly, the increasing volume of international travel and the need for enhanced border security measures are driving the adoption of e-gates across the globe. The implementation of e-gates not only improves passenger flow but also reduces operational costs for airports and border control agencies.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the adoption of e-gates as they offer contactless and hygienic solutions for identity verification, reducing the risk of disease transmission.
