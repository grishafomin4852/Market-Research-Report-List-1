<p><p>Foliar feeding is a method of supplying nutrients to plants by spraying a liquid fertilizer solution onto their leaves. Firstly, the growing need to enhance crop productivity and yield has increased the adoption of foliar feeding techniques across the agricultural industry. Farmers are increasingly recognizing the benefits of foliar feeding in improving nutrient availability and utilization, ultimately leading to higher yields.</p><p>Additionally, foliar feeding offers a more targeted approach to deliver specific nutrients, such as micronutrients, to plants. This has gained traction in horticulture, where specialized nutrient formulations can address specific deficiencies and boost plant health.</p><p>The market has also been driven by the increasing demand for organic farming practices. Foliar feeding provides an efficient way to deliver organic fertilizers and bio-stimulants, catering to the needs of organic farmers who aim to minimize the use of synthetic chemicals.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in spray technology and increased awareness about the benefits of foliar feeding among farmers have contributed to market growth. The development of precision spraying equipment and accurate nutrient formulations has improved the effectiveness of foliar feeding, further driving its adoption.</p><p>The foliar feeding market is expected to continue its growth trajectory in the coming years.
