<p><p>Ceramic catalyst substrates are used in catalytic converters, which are integral components of automotive exhaust systems. As governments around the world implement stricter emission norms to reduce air pollution, the demand for catalytic converters is expected to increase, which in turn will drive the demand for ceramic catalyst substrates. Additionally, the growing adoption of electric vehicles is likely to create significant opportunities for the market, as these vehicles still require catalytic converters to minimize emissions from their internal combustion range extenders or fuel cells.</p><p>In terms of the current outlook, the market is witnessing steady growth. Moreover, the use of lightweight ceramic materials allows for better thermal management and reduces the overall weight of the catalytic converter, thereby enhancing fuel efficiency.</p><p>Overall, with the increasing focus on environmental sustainability and the enforcement of stringent emission norms, the ceramic catalyst substrate market is expected to exhibit steady growth in the coming years.
