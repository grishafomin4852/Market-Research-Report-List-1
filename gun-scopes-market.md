<p><p>Gun scopes can be divided into two main market types: optical and mechanical scopes. Optical scopes primarily rely on lenses and mirrors to magnify the target and enhance accuracy. They are designed to provide clear and precise images, allowing shooters to better aim and hit their targets. On the other hand, mechanical scopes utilize internal mechanisms and adjustments to assist shooters in compensating for factors such as elevation, windage, and parallax. These scopes offer greater control and fine-tuning options for more accurate shooting.</p></p>
