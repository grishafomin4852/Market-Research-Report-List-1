<p><p>The automotive premium tires market can be categorized into two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires have multiple layers of rubber-coated cords crisscrossing at angles, providing enhanced stability and durability. Radial tires, on the other hand, have cords positioned at a 90-degree angle to the tread, allowing for improved grip, better fuel efficiency, and smoother rides. Both types aim to enhance the performance and safety of vehicles, but they differ in terms of construction and benefits offered to drivers.</p></p>
