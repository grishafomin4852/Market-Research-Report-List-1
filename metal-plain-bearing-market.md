<p><p>Metal plain bearings, also known as sleeve bearings or bushings, are mechanical components used to reduce friction between rotating shafts and stationary support structures. They consist of a cylindrical metal sleeve, often made of materials like bronze or steel, which is typically lubricated to minimize wear and friction.</p><p>The metal plain bearing market is witnessing steady growth due to the increasing demand from various industrial applications such as automotive, machinery, aerospace, and construction. These bearings offer several benefits, including high load capacity, low friction, high durability, and resistance to extreme temperatures and harsh environments. This makes them suitable for applications where high reliability and performance are crucial.</p><p>The market analysis of the metal plain bearing industry reveals positive growth prospects. Factors driving this growth include the rising demand for automotive vehicles, increasing industrialization in emerging economies, and the growing emphasis on energy-efficient technologies.
