<p><p>Mild steel billet refers to a semi-finished steel product that is commonly used in the construction and manufacturing industries. It is a square or rectangular cross-sectioned steel billet with a width and height between 50mm to 200mm. Mild steel billets are primarily made by continuously casting liquid steel into moulds of various sizes and then allowing it to solidify.</p><p>The mild steel billet market is witnessing steady growth due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing demand for construction projects, particularly in emerging economies, is driving the market. The construction industry relies heavily on mild steel billets for the production of various structural components. Additionally, the growing manufacturing sector, particularly in the automotive and transportation industries, is further fueling the demand for mild steel billets.</p><p>Moreover, the market is benefiting from technological advancements in steel production methods, such as the adoption of electric arc furnaces and continuous casting processes. These advancements have increased the efficiency and reduced the production costs of mild steel billets, making them more affordable for consumers and manufacturers.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is also seeing the trend of product differentiation and customization.
