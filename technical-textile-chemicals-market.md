<p><p>Technical textile chemicals market is classified into three types, namely auxiliaries, colorants, and others. Auxiliaries are chemicals used in the pre-treatment and finishing processes of textiles, providing functionalities like softening, sizing, and anti-static properties. Colorants are chemicals used for dyeing and printing textiles to achieve desired colors and patterns. Other chemicals include flame retardants, water repellents, and antimicrobials, among others, which enhance the performance and functionality of technical textiles. These chemicals play a crucial role in improving the physical and chemical properties of technical textiles, making them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
