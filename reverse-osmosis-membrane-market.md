<p><strong>The Reverse Osmosis Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellulose-Based Membranes</li><li>Thin Film Composite Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reverse osmosis membrane market types mainly include cellulose-based membranes and thin film composite membranes. </p><p>Cellulose-based membranes are made from natural cellulose material, such as wood pulp. These membranes have good chemical and thermal stability, but they have lower rejection rates compared to thin film composite membranes.</p><p>Thin film composite membranes, on the other hand, are made by depositing thin layers of polyamide and polysulfone on a support layer. These membranes offer higher rejection rates, improved durability, and higher permeability, making them the more widely used and preferred choice in reverse osmosis applications.</p></p>
