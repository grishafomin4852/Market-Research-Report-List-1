<p><p>Lenticular sheets are specialized plastic sheets that are used in the production of lenticular lenses. Lenticular lenses are used to create images that have an illusion of depth or motion when viewed from different angles. The sheets have rows of small convex lenses, known as lenticules, on one side, which allow for the creation of these visual effects.</p><p>The lenticular sheet market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period. One of the driving factors for this growth is the increasing demand for unique and attractive packaging solutions in various industries, such as advertising, cosmetics, and food & beverage. Lenticular sheets are used in the production of autostereoscopic 3D displays, which provide a glasses-free 3D viewing experience. With the increasing popularity of 3D movies and games, the demand for such displays is expected to drive the market for lenticular sheets.</p><p>In terms of regional analysis, North America has dominated the lenticular sheet market due to the high adoption of advanced packaging technologies and the presence of major players in the region.
