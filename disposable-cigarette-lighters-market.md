Additionally, the growing awareness about the health hazards of smoking has led to a shift from traditional cigarette lighters to disposable ones, which are considered safer due to their child-lock mechanism and absence of an exposed flame.</p><p>Moreover, the surge in outdoor activities, camping, and social gatherings where smoking is prevalent has also contributed to the market's growth.
