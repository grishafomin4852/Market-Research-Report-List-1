<p><p>The car plastic tailgate market consists of two main types: thermoplastic tailgates and thermoset tailgates. Thermoplastic tailgates are made from a material that can be melted and reshaped multiple times, allowing for efficient manufacturing and repair processes. On the other hand, thermoset tailgates are made from a material that undergoes a one-time curing process, resulting in a strong and durable final product. The choice between these types depends on factors such as cost, manufacturing efficiency, and desired durability.</p></p>
