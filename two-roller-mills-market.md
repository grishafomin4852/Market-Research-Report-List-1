<p><p>Two-roller mills are used in the milling industry to crush and grind grains into powders. There are two main types: horizontal roller mill and vertical roller mill. Horizontal roller mills have two parallel rollers that rotate in opposite directions, providing a more efficient and consistent milling process. On the other hand, vertical roller mills have a single large roller that grinds the material against a stationary surface, offering a different milling approach. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different milling needs in various industries.</p></p>
