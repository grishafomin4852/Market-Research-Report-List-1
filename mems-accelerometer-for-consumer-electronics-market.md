<p><p>The MEMS accelerometer market for consumer electronics consists of various types. The 1-axis MEMS accelerometer measures acceleration in a single direction, while the 2-axis accelerometer measures in two perpendicular directions. The 3-axis accelerometer can measure acceleration in any direction, offering greater versatility. Moving further, the 6-axis accelerometer combines the 3-axis accelerometer with a 3-axis gyroscope to measure rotational motion. Lastly, the 9-axis accelerometer combines a 6-axis accelerometer with a 3-axis magnetometer to detect orientation and magnetic fields. These different types cater to the diverse needs of consumer electronics applications.</p></p>
