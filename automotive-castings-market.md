<p><ul><li>Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Squeeze Die Casting</li><li>Semi-Solid Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive castings market includes various types of casting processes, such as pressure die casting, vacuum die casting, squeeze die casting, and semi-solid die casting. Pressure die casting involves injecting molten metal into a mold under high pressure. Vacuum die casting uses a vacuum to draw molten metal into the mold. Squeeze die casting involves applying pressure to the mold during solidification. Semi-solid die casting utilizes partially solidified metal to improve casting quality. These processes are widely used in the automotive industry for manufacturing various components and parts.</p></p>
