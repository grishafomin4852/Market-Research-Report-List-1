<p><p>A multilayer flexible printed circuit board (PCB) is a PCB that consists of multiple layers of conductive copper traces and insulating layers, all sandwiched between flexible dielectric materials. These boards offer several advantages over rigid PCBs, including improved flexibility, reduced space requirements, and higher resistance to vibrations and shocks. Due to their excellent electrical performance and durability, multilayer flexible PCBs are widely used in various applications such as consumer electronics, automotive, healthcare, and telecommunications.</p><p>In terms of the market analysis, the multilayer flexible PCB market is expected to experience substantial growth during the forecast period.
